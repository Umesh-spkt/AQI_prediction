Welcome To Air Quality Prediction System

Air pollution is considered to be one of the biggest environmental threats. Air Quality Prediction System has sole purpose of providing insights about daily air quality. We have used Machine learning approach to predict ambient air quality with the help of air quality data. We have obtained ground data sets from Department of Hydrology and Meteorology which were used in Prediction of Air Quality Index.

Our Predict AQI page can successfully predict Air Quality Index of Kathmandu City with reasonable accuracy. Particulate matters in air is our primary feature to predict the air quality. We have used PM 10, PM 2.5, O3 to predict Air Quality Index. The predicted AQI value can be compared against the category table below to Know quality of air. One can access the past data of respective parameters from our Past data page to study trend of air pollution level in previous years.
